Caponata Pasta

An easy vegetarian caponata pasta ready to go in just 20 minutes.

It's simple to make and packs three of your five-a-day into one delicious meal!

Ingredients List

  1. 4 tbsp olive oil
  2. 1 large onion, finely chopped
  3. 4 garlic cloves, finely sliced
  4. 250g chargrilled Mediterranean veg
  5. 400g can chopped tomatoes
  6. 1 tbsp small capers
  7. 2 tbsp raisins
  8. 350g rigatoni, penne or another short pasta shape
  9. bunch basil leaves, picked
  10. parmesan (or vegetarian alternative), shaved, to serve
