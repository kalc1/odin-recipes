Southern Mac and Cheese

Description

The comfort food you need sometimes. With a southern twist.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb macaroni noodles
  • 8 oz mozzarella, cubed

  • 2 oz parmesan, grated
  • 3 eggs
  • 4 oz yellow cheddar, shredded
  • 4 oz white cheddar, shredded
